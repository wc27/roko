Time Complexity (Fractional Knapsack)
Step 1: Sort items by value/weight ratio

Sorting takes:

O(n log n)

Step 2: Loop through sorted items and fill knapsack

This takes:

O(n)

Final Time Complexity:
O(n log n)


(Sorting dominates the complexity)
